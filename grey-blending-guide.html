What is Grey Blending?

Grey blending is a hair coloring technique that softens the contrast between your natural grey hair and your dyed hair. Instead of full coverage (which creates a harsh regrowth line), grey blending uses highlights, lowlights, and specialized techniques to create a natural blend.

Grey Blending Options

Highlights for Grey Blending

Adding blonde or silver highlights throughout the hair camouflages grey and creates dimension. Best for 30-50% grey.

Maintenance: Every 8-12 weeks

Lowlights for Grey Blending

Adding darker pieces creates depth and helps grey hair look intentional. Great for naturally dark hair going grey.

Maintenance: Every 8-12 weeks

Grey Blending Balayage

Hand-painted highlights that blend seamlessly with grey. Creates a lived-in, natural look with very graceful grow-out.

Maintenance: Every 12-16 weeks

Full Transition to Grey

Strategically lifting and toning to help you go fully grey/silver without an awkward grow-out phase.

Timeline: 3-12 months depending on starting color

Am I a Good Candidate for Grey Blending?

Grey blending works best if you:

  • Have 20-70% grey hair
  • Want to reduce maintenance time/costs
  • Are tired of the constant root touch-up cycle
  • Want a more natural, lived-in look
  • Are ready to embrace your natural grey

What to Tell Your Colorist

During your consultation, discuss:

  • Your long-term goals (blend grey vs. go fully grey)
  • Your current hair color history
  • How much maintenance you're comfortable with
  • Whether you're open to multiple sessions
