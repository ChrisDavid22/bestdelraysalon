Money-Saving Tips

  • 1. Ask about package deals - Many salons offer discounts for cut + color together
  • 2. Consider maintenance visits - A gloss between full highlights extends your color
  • 3. Choose low-maintenance styles - Balayage grows out more gracefully than foils
  • 4. Don't go cheap on color - A bad dye job costs more to fix than doing it right initially
  • 5. Ask about new client specials - Many salons offer discounts for first visits
  • 6. Follow aftercare instructions - Proper maintenance makes color last longer
