Planning Your Wedding Hair: Your wedding hair is one of the most photographed elements of your day. Start booking 3-6 months in advance for popular stylists, and always schedule a trial run.

Wedding Hair Timeline

6+ Months Before: Research & Book

Start researching stylists. Look at portfolios, read reviews specifically mentioning bridal work. Book your stylist once you've found the right fit.

3-4 Months Before: Hair Trial

Schedule your trial run. Bring inspiration photos, your veil/headpiece, and be honest about what you like and don't like.

2-4 Weeks Before: Color Appointment

If you're getting color done, schedule it 2-4 weeks before the wedding - fresh enough to look vibrant but with time to adjust if needed.

1-2 Days Before: Any Treatments

Keratin treatments, deep conditioning, or blowouts should be done 1-2 days before (not day-of).

Florida-Specific Wedding Hair Tips

Humidity-Proofing Your Style

Florida's humidity can destroy wedding hair in minutes. Here's how to fight it:

  • Anti-humidity spray: Essential. Your stylist should apply before and after styling.
  • Texture spray: Helps grip and hold last longer.
  • Secure everything: More bobby pins than you think necessary.
  • Consider timing: Indoor AC ceremony = more style options. Outdoor = plan for humidity.
  • Keratin treatment: 2-4 weeks before for frizz-free guarantee.
